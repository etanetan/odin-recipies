Egg In A Hole

This is a recipe to make the classic egg in a hole meal that tastes so great!

This is my favorite recipe for making eggs. You get a good amount of fats, carbs, and protein.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ teaspoons bacon grease
  • 1 slice bread
  • 1 egg
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Melt the bacon grease in a non-stick pan over low heat.
  2. When the side facing down is lightly toasted, about 2 minutes, flip and crack the egg into the hole; season with salt and pepper.
  3. Continue to cook until the egg is cooked and mostly firm.
  4. Flip again and cook 1 minute more to assure doneness on both sides. Serve immediately.
